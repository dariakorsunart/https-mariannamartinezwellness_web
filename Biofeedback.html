What is Biofeedback System?

A Biofeedback System is an advanced technology that scans the body by sending frequencies and analyzing responses through galvanic skin resistance. This process helps detect patterns related to stress, allergies, toxicity, organ imbalances, food sensitivities, nutritional deficiencies, and other dysfunctions.

According to research from the Mayo Clinic, biofeedback is used to help manage a variety of physical and mental health conditions, including:

  • Anxiety and stress
  • Asthma
  • ADHD (Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder)
  • Chemotherapy side effects
  • Chronic pain
  • Constipation
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Headaches
  • High blood pressure
  • Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS)
  • Tinnitus (ringing in the ears)
  • Stroke recovery
  • TMJ (temporomandibular joint disorder)
  • Urinary incontinence
